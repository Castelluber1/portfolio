My Process

1. Intake

I begin by aligning on goals and clarifying priorities to ensure resources are allocated effectively for maximum impact.

2. Plan

I design detailed research plans, using participant screeners and discussion guides to tackle challenges, reduce risks, and deliver impactful project outcomes as scheduled.

3. Conduct

I engage participants through thoughtful questions and observations, capturing both verbal and non-verbal cues to gain a comprehensive understanding of their needs and experiences.

4. Analyze

I transform data into actionable insights by identifying trends and crafting presentations catered to stakeholder's needs to inform design decisions.

5. Delivery

I tailor deliverables to each audience—detailed decks for designers, concise summaries for stakeholders, and one-pagers for executives—ensuring clear communication at every level.

6. Documentation

I document all findings in an organized research repository, enhancing accessibility and ensuring the team can easily leverage valuable insights.
