My Process

1. Intake

I take the time to really understand what's needed, making sure I clarify goals to allocate resources efficiently

2. Plan

I create resources like screeners and guides, strategizing in advance to effectively address challenges and achieve objectives.

3. Conduct

Asking question and carefully observing both their verbal and non-verbal cues gathering evidence to truly understand their needs and experiences, paving the way for impactful solutions

4. Analyze

This is where I analyze user information to uncover insights, reviewing everything to identify trends. Crafting a concise summary for the final deliverable is always challenging, as I must carefully select content to ensure clarity and relevance for stakeholders.

5. Delivery

Beginning with a thorough deck, I progressively refine it for clarity and relevance, tailoring information to diverse audience needs: detailed decks for designers, concise summaries for stakeholders, and one-pagers for executives.

6. Documentation

I gather all project information and organize it into a Research repository, a platform I personally coded within our Confluence environment. This enhances accessibility for designers and ensures all team members have easy access to valuable research insights.
